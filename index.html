Our Weather

The weather in Switzerland is moderate all year, no extra heat or cold.

Our Capital

The capital of Switzerland is Bern. The population of Bern is around 450,000. Albert Einstein lived here for a few years!

Location

Our country is landlocked country in Central Europe. It is surrounded by France, Germany, Austria and Italy.

Our Attractions

  • Matterhorn

Enjoy the fresh mountain air at Matterhorn, reaching an amazing 4,478 meters tall. Nothing but a beautiful view, and crisp mountain air! The tallest in the Alps, don't miss it!

Cost: 0 CHF (0 CAD)




  • Rhine Falls

The Rhine Falls are an amazing 150 meters span falls, the largest in Central Europe. The best time to visit is in June and July, when the snow melts and the falls swell.

Cost: 5 CHF (About 7 CAD)

  • Lake Lugano and Ticino

Lake Lugano lies on the Swiss-Italian border.

Cost: 0 CHF (0 CAD)
